Been thinking about Camila all week. She gave her two weeks notice on Monday. Very professional, wrote a nice goodbye message to the Slack channel about "growth opportunities" and "grateful for the journey." Classic corporate speak, which is funny because we always said we weren't going to be that kind of company.

I keep replaying her words about stability. We've been sprinting for three years. I remember when Felipe slept in the office for a month during the pilot launch. The Series B is done and instead of feeling like we've arrived somewhere, it feels like we're just getting permission to sprint for another three years. And now I'm supposed to hire someone to replace Camila, someone who'll probably leave too when they realize what the pace is actually like.

My mom asked me on Sunday's call why I sounded tired. I said we just closed a big funding round. She said "mijo, that was two weeks ago, shouldn't you be happy by now?" I didn't know how to answer that.
